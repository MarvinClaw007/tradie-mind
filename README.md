## 💡 The Solution

### Core Features

#### 1. Voice-First Quoting
- Record voice note on-site
- AI transcribes, prices, generates branded PDF quote
- **Under 2 minutes** (vs 30+ minutes manual)
- **85% faster** than traditional quoting

#### 2. AI Scheduling & Dispatch
- Automatic job scheduling by location, urgency, availability
- Instant cancellation filling
- **25% reduction** in drive time
- **+3 extra jobs per week**

#### 3. Autonomous Invoicing
- Job done = invoice sent automatically
- Payment chasers at 7, 14, 21 days
- Xero & MYOB sync
- BAS prepared quarterly
- **40% faster payment collection**

#### 4. 24/7 AI Phone Answering
- Answers calls while you're on the tools
- Qualifies leads and books jobs
- Australian accent & terminology
- Professional receptionist experience

#### 5. Compliance & Accounting
- Auto-generate SWMS, site reports, warranty docs
- Xero/MYOB automatic sync
- GST applied correctly
- Quarterly BAS preparation

## 📋 Technical Specification

### Architecture
```
TradieMind AI
├── Mobile App (React Native)
│   ├── Voice recording & transcription
│   ├── Offline mode
│   └── Push notifications
├── Web Dashboard (Next.js)
│   ├── Admin panel
│   ├── Analytics
│   └── Team management
├── AI Engine (Python/FastAPI)
│   ├── Speech-to-text (Whisper)
│   ├── Natural language processing
│   ├── Quote generation
│   └── Scheduling optimization
├── Integrations
│   ├── Xero/MYOB
│   ├── Google Calendar
```
