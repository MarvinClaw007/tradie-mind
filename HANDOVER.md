## 🎨 Brand Guidelines

### Colors
- **Primary:** `#7c5dff` (Purple - trust, tech)
- **Secondary:** `#ff6b6b` (Coral - action, urgency)
- **Background:** `#1a1a2e` (Dark navy - professional)
- **Text:** `#ffffff` / `#e0e0e0`

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter or Poppins (bold, friendly)
- **Body:** Inter or Open Sans (clean, readable)

### Tone of Voice
- **Friendly but professional** — mate, but not too casual
- **Action-oriented** — "Get it done", "Too easy"
- **Australian** — local terminology, no Americanisms
- **No jargon** — explain AI simply

### Logo Ideas
1. Speech bubble + wrench icon
2. "TE" monogram with voice wave
3. Checkmark + tool silhouette
4. Simple wordmark: "tooeasy.me"
